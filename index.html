How to Reset Your StarRez Password

📱 StarRez👤 James Okafor📅 10 May 2026⏱ 3 min read

Overview

StarRez uses Single Sign-On (SSO) via your university Microsoft 365 account. Your StarRez password is the same as your university network password.

Steps

  1. Visit aka.ms/sspr to reset your university password.
  2. Follow the self-service password reset prompts.
  3. Once reset, return to StarRez and log in with your new credentials.

Still Locked Out?

Contact the IT Service Desk on ext. 1234 or email servicedesk@university.ac.uk.

Raising a Maintenance Work Order in Evolution

📱 Evolution (MRI)👤 James Okafor📅 22 Apr 2026⏱ 5 min read

Overview

Any member of staff can raise a maintenance work order in Evolution. This guide walks you through the process.

Steps

  1. Log in to Evolution at evolution.university.ac.uk.
  2. Click New Work Order from the dashboard.
  3. Select the affected building and location from the drop-down menus.
  4. Describe the fault clearly in the description field.
  5. Select the category (Electrical, Plumbing, General, etc.).
  6. Attach a photo if helpful.
  7. Click Submit.

You will receive an email confirmation with a work order reference number.

QlikView Dashboard Not Loading — Troubleshooting Guide

📱 QlikView👤 Calbert Williams📅 28 Mar 2026⏱ 4 min read

Symptoms

  • Dashboard shows blank / spinning wheel
  • Error: "Could not connect to QlikView server"
  • Login page loops without authenticating

Resolution Steps

  1. Clear your browser cache — Ctrl+Shift+Delete → Cached images and files → Clear.
  2. Try a different browser — QlikView is optimised for Chrome and Edge.
  3. Check system status — data@university.ac.uk sends a notification if QlikView is offline.
  4. VPN — If working remotely, ensure you are connected to the university VPN.

Escalation

If none of the above resolve your issue, email data@university.ac.uk with your dashboard name and a screenshot of any error message.

QlikView to Qlik Sense Migration — What You Need to Know

📱 QlikView👤 Dr. Priya Sharma📅 5 May 2026⏱ 3 min read

Background

The IT Business Systems and Data teams are evaluating a migration from QlikView to Qlik Sense. This article summarises the current position and what it means for users.

Current Status

A business case is being prepared. No migration date has been confirmed. Existing QlikView dashboards remain fully operational.
